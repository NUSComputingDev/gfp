A LITTLE ABOUT THE EVENT

An occasion to celebrate your university journey in NUS. This event is packed with F.U.N, representing Fantastic, Unforgettable and Nostalgic! During the 4 hours event, all you have to do is to enjoy yourself! Play some games, sing some songs, take some photos or simply chat with one another!

This year event is themed Carnival Across Time (CAT), sounds familiar? Just like SOCCAT :D The story goes, in this universe of ours, there is this mysterious carnival created by the game master that travels from planet to planet to search for players to win off his prize stash! Hence, this year, the carnival arrives on Earth and fortunately, we are the lucky ones who have been chosen to participate!
